# Default Clinic Knowledge Base

This is the default knowledge base template. Each clinic can have their own customized version.

## Hours of Operation
- Monday to Friday: 8:00am - 6:00pm
- Saturday: 8:00am - 12:00pm
- Sunday: Closed
- Public Holidays: Closed

## Location & Parking
- Free on-site parking available for patients
- Street parking also available nearby
- The clinic is wheelchair accessible
- Located on the ground floor with easy access

## Pricing
- Initial Consultation (45-60 min): $80 - $120
- Standard Adjustment (15-20 min): $55 - $75
- Extended Consultation (30 min): $70 - $90
- We accept cash, card, and EFTPOS
- HICAPS available for instant health fund rebates

## First Visit Information
- Please arrive 10-15 minutes early to complete paperwork
- Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
- Bring any relevant X-rays, scans, or medical reports
- Initial consultation includes full history and assessment
- The appointment takes approximately 45-60 minutes
- Treatment may begin on the first visit if appropriate

## Services Offered
- Chiropractic adjustments and spinal manipulation
- Soft tissue therapy and massage
- Dry needling
- Rehabilitation exercises
- Posture assessment and correction
- Sports injury treatment
- Pregnancy care
- Pediatric chiropractic

## Insurance & Payment
- We accept all major health funds
- HICAPS machine for instant rebates
- Medicare does not cover chiropractic services
- DVA and WorkCover claims accepted (with prior approval)
- Payment plans available for treatment packages

## Cancellation Policy
- Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations
- Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee
- We understand emergencies happen - please call us
- Rescheduling is always welcome with adequate notice

## What to Expect
- Friendly, professional staff
- Clean, modern clinic environment
- Private treatment rooms
- Thorough explanation of your condition
- Personalized treatment plans
- Home care advice and exercises

## Contact Information
- For urgent matters, please call our main number
- After hours emergencies: Call 000 or visit your local hospital
- We aim to respond to inquiries within 24 hours
