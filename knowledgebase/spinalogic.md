# Spinalogic Clinic Knowledge Base

## Hours of Operation
- Monday to Friday: 8:00am - 6:00pm
- Saturday: 9:00am - 1:00pm
- Sunday: Closed
- Public Holidays: Closed

## Location & Address
- Shop 2, 123 Health Street, Brisbane QLD 4000
- Located near Brisbane Central Station
- Easy access from public transport

## Parking
- Free underground parking available for patients
- Enter from Health Street rear entrance
- Street parking also available (2-hour limit)
- The clinic is wheelchair accessible

## Pricing & Fees
- Initial Consultation (45-60 min): $95
- Standard Adjustment (15-20 min): $65
- Extended Consultation (30 min): $80
- We accept cash, card, and EFTPOS
- HICAPS available for instant health fund rebates

## First Visit Information
- Please arrive 10-15 minutes early to complete paperwork
- Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
- Bring any relevant X-rays, scans, or medical reports
- Initial consultation includes full history and assessment
- The appointment takes approximately 45-60 minutes
- Treatment may begin on the first visit if appropriate

## Services Offered
- Chiropractic adjustments and spinal manipulation
- Soft tissue therapy and remedial massage
- Dry needling and acupuncture
- Rehabilitation exercises
- Posture assessment and correction
- Sports injury treatment
- Pregnancy care
- Pediatric chiropractic
- Workplace ergonomic assessments

## Insurance & Payment
- We accept all major health funds
- HICAPS machine for instant rebates
- Medicare does not cover chiropractic services
- DVA and WorkCover claims accepted (with prior approval)
- Payment plans available for treatment packages

## Cancellation Policy
- Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations
- Late cancellations may incur a $40 fee
- No-shows may incur the full consultation fee
- Rescheduling is always welcome with adequate notice
