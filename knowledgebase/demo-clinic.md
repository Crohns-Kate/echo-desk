## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is chiropractic safe?
Yes, chiropractic care is very safe when performed by a registered chiropractor. We'll assess your suitability before any treatment.

### Do I need a referral?
No referral is needed to see a chiropractor. You can book directly.

### How many visits will I need?
This varies by condition. We'll give you an honest estimate after your first assessment. Many people feel improvement within 2-4 visits.

### Can I claim on Medicare?
Medicare doesn't cover chiropractic. However, you may be eligible if you have a chronic disease management plan from your GP.

### Do you treat children?
Yes! We have experience with pediatric chiropractic care. Techniques are modified to be gentle and appropriate.

### I'm pregnant - can I still come?
Absolutely! Chiropractic care can help with pregnancy-related back pain. We have special pillows and techniques for pregnant patients.
